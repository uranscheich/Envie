# envie

**From craving to checklist.**

envie is an iOS app for families, couples, and shared households who want to eat together without renegotiating dinner every single day.

The app connects recipe import, shared decision-making, weekly planning, grocery lists, and cooking mode in one flow: bring recipes in, choose together, plan the week, shop, and cook.

## Idea

Recipes are usually scattered everywhere: browser tabs, screenshots, chats, photo libraries, saved posts, and notes. envie turns those loose recipe ideas into something usable.

It does not try to be just another recipe archive. The core question is much more practical:

> What are we eating today — and what are we planning for the week?

## Core Features

- **Recipe import** from the web, screenshots, or photos
- **OCR and Apple Intelligence** to structure ingredients and cooking steps
- **Cookbook** for saved, editable recipes
- **Swipe modes** for quick decisions or free discovery
- **Weekly plan** for jointly selected meals
- **Grocery list** generated from planned recipes
- **Cooking mode** with large steps, ingredient view, and timers
- **Contexts** for Local, Family, and Friends

## Contexts

envie separates decisions by context, because not every meal is planned with the same people.

### Local

The simple starting point: local profiles on one device. Useful for couples, families, or shared households who want to try envie immediately without setup friction.

### Family

The household context for families, couples, and shared households. It is designed around shared planning, a common weekly plan, and groceries for everyday life.

### Friends

A dedicated group mode for cooking nights, visitors, or friend groups. This is not about weekly planning. It is about finding one meal that a group can agree on.

## Product Flow

1. Import or create a recipe.
2. Save it in the cookbook.
3. Decide together through swipe sessions.
4. Add shared matches to the weekly plan.
5. Generate and check off the grocery list.
6. Cook with a focused step-by-step mode.
